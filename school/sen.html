SEN Policy

We are committed to providing an inclusive environment where all students can thrive. Our SEN policy outlines our approach to supporting students with additional needs.

Support Structure

Our SEN department is led by our SENCO, Mrs. J. Smith. We offer:

  • In-class support
  • Small group interventions
  • 1-to-1 mentoring
  • External agency partnerships

Accessibility

Our campus is fully accessible with:

  • Wheelchair ramps
  • Lifts in all buildings
  • Accessible toilets
  • Hearing loop systems
